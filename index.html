My name is J. Gabriel Bruce. I have a degree in Biomedical Biology and Biochemistry. I am also a certified Front End Software Developer. I live in Grimes, Iowa with my wife, daughter, and crazy German Shepherd.

I am currently working as the Hospital Services Department Lead for a large blood center in Iowa. I coordinate a team of over 50 employees in charge of blood reference, laboratory testing, component manufacturing, and product management.

I love working with computers and programming. I'm always trying to learn new things.
